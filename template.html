Sioux Falls Market Fundamentals

Population Growth

The Sioux Falls metro area had an estimated population of 264,552 in 2018, which is a 17.4% increase since 2010. It is projected to add another 20,000 people by 2023.1

Strong Employment

The Sioux Falls metro area unemployment rate is currently 2.5%, which is well below the national rate of 4%.2

Macroeconomic Insulation

During the Great Recession, Sioux Falls’ economy fared well despite the harsh realities of the global downturn. When the national unemployment rate peaked at 10% in October 2009, it was just 4.7% in Sioux Falls.2

Market Diversity

Sioux Falls’ economy is not reliant on any one sector. The largest industry in the area is healthcare, which accounts for 20% of the workforce, while retail, manufacturing, and finance each account for approximately 10% of the workforce.2
